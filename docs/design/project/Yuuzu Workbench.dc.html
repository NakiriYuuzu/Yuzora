Features

Everything in one window.

No tab-juggling between six apps. Yuuzu folds your whole workflow into one calm, native surface.

SpacesEach repo is a Space with its own files, branches and theme. Switch in a keystroke.
Git that keeps upA live commit graph, inline diffs and a console — stage, push and rebase without leaving.
Terminals & SSHSplit terminals, jump into SSH and SFTP on your servers — all docked where you work.
Agents that actConnect ACP agents that read your repo, propose diffs and wait for your approval.
